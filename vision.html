    What each module needs

    Working now

    SAM assistant

    Multilingual prompts, editable controls, local preferences and the manual Microsoft Forms workflow already work.

    Approval + pilot

    PPE photo check

    Use an approved on-device vision model, no identity matching, no stored photo and a manual fallback. Validate accuracy before it affects access.

    App service

    Scheduled reminders

    Reliable iPhone background reminders need a native app or an approved push service, notification consent and completion status.

    iClean + map data

    Route planning

    Needs room coordinates, access paths, task priority, estimated duration, laundry stops and a supported iClean data feed.

    Operations data

    Room allocation

    Needs shift availability, room status, workload rules and zones. Optimisation proposes a plan; the supervisor can always override it.

    Guardrails for a real pilot
    • No facial recognition or hidden worker scoring.
    • No automatic safety or room-allocation decision without review.
    • Use only approved SWP, HSEQ and operational data.
    • Define photo retention, privacy ownership and support before rollout.
