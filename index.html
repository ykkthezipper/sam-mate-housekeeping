About this pilot

Safer completion, better understanding

SAM Mate is an independent learning aid. It does not replace training, an SWP, supervisor instructions, or your own check of today's conditions.

Why I care

I built SAM Mate after seeing colleagues complete daily SAMs in a language they were still learning. The goal is to reduce repetitive typing, support safer completion, and help every worker understand the hazards and controls they submit.

This independent pilot is inspired by care for people, courage to improve a difficult process, and curiosity to find a better and safer way. It is not an official company system or endorsement.

No account is required. Your name, templates, and recent task history stay in this browser. Nothing is uploaded by this tool.
